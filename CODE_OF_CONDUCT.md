# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We want ShortcutGenius to be a respectful, technically serious, and welcoming project.

Examples of expected behavior:

- be constructive and specific
- assume good intent, but be clear about problems
- focus criticism on code, docs, and behavior, not people
- help new contributors understand project constraints

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- doxxing or sharing private information
- intentionally disruptive behavior
- public posting of secrets or security-sensitive material

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

## Reporting

Use the contact path in `SECURITY.md` for sensitive reports. For general conduct issues, contact the maintainers privately instead of escalating in public threads.
